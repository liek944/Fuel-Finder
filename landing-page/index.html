THE PROBLEM

Fuel Prices Are Unpredictable.
Your Wallet Pays the Price.

Every day, Filipino drivers waste time and money guessing which station has the best price. There's no reliable way to compare — you just drive in, hope for the best, and fill up.

No Price Visibility

Fuel prices change weekly, but stations don't advertise them. You only find out when you're already at the pump.

Wasted Time & Fuel

Driving to multiple stations to compare prices costs you the very thing you're trying to save — fuel.

No Trusted Source

Online fuel price lists are outdated, incomplete, or based in Manila. Nothing reflects your local market.

THE SOLUTION

One App. Every Station.
Real Prices.

Fuel Finder uses real-time, community-reported prices and smart geolocation to show you exactly where to fill up — and navigates you there.

  • See real fuel prices reported by drivers like you
  • Find the nearest and cheapest station in seconds
  • Get turn-by-turn directions powered by OSRM
FEATURES

Everything You Need to
Fuel Smarter

Built for the real world. Powered by PostGIS, OSRM, and a community of drivers.

Nearby Stations, Instantly

Geospatial search finds stations within your radius using PostGIS. See them pinned on a live map.

Turn-by-Turn Routing

Get the fastest route to your chosen station with OSRM-powered A* navigation.

Compare Before You Fill

View and filter by fuel type — Diesel, Premium, Regular — and price. Community-verified.

Prices by Real Drivers

Anyone can report current prices. Verified by the community for accuracy you can trust.

Owner Portal

Multi-tenant portal for station owners to manage listings, update prices, and view analytics.

PWA-Ready

Install on your home screen. Key features work even without internet connection.
